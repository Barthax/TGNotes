Hiscores

Hiscores shown here are only ones people have send me and wanted me to list here for others to see. This is simply a place where you can publish your all time best score in a pinball simulation, a score you are really proud of!!! And then others do the same and push you down the list and you begin playing the pinball again to beat that idiot who pushed you down.

The hiscore list is now as of June 2002 beeing moderated by Rick Maultra who will keep it up to date and add new tables.
