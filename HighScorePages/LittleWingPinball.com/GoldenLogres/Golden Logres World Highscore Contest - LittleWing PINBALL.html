Golden Logres World Highscore Contest Results
Thank you very much for participating in the Goden Logres Highscore Contest. We were deligted to receive so many awsome scores --- they were far beyond our expectataions!
And now, meet the Golden Logres champion, Mr. Fred van der Zwet who won the contest with the amazing score of 9,935,922,900.
CHAMPION !: Fred van der Zwet SCORE: 9,935,922,900

After playing Golden Logres the first time, I knew: "This game is mine." I was always interested in the character and world of King Arthur and his knights, and of course in the stories about Excalibur, the Round Table, Merlin, Camelot, Avalon and the Holy Grail.

The game is fascinating, because all those elements are in it. It is also fascinating because of the different tables and missions. Therefor it is addicting in a way. I played the game a lot of times, and every time it took me more hours to complete it. The duration of the final winning game was about eight hours. I am very proud being the number one, either I am convinced my score can be beaten (by myself or perhaps by somebody else).

I recommend everybody who likes to play real pinball games, to try the Little Wing computer games. I also play the other Little Wing pinball games, and all of them are very real and genuine in their own way (unfortunately, Little Wing does not pay me to say this, so it is the truth). I wish a lot of pleasure and good luck to all players of Golden Logres.

Fred van der Zwet, Ferwert, The Netherlands.
The third Prize: Alan Wahlers SCORE: 2,775,949,100

It must be quite some time since Golden Logres occupied so much of my time. Over the period I greatly enjoyed playing your excellent creation, spurred on by the mini-competition with my son Christopher (who you will see from the contest results finished not far behind me).

Longer ago, I particularly remember my time after school in my youth when, meeting with friends at the local coffee bar, many happy times were spent on the real pinball machines ( I am talking 1960's here). GL and your other pinball games give a very realistic play ..............almost like the real thing!

I am sure my work as an architect here in England must have suffered at the time that I was heavily involved with GL. If you are ever looking to establish a new HQ for Littlewing here in England I can recommend a good architect!
The game was very informative as well and I now know well the legend of King Arthur.

My congratulations go to the runaway winner of the competition and I would also like to say hello to the many other contestants. I was looking for an old screenshot of the one time I was actually leading the contest, but couldn't find it. I have included a photo to prove the tee shirt actually arrived.

I must confess that I have only tried the demo version of JZ, but see that it is very popular all over the World, and particularly in Japan! I am slightly wary of buying a copy lest I spend too much time enjoying myself, but then again...........
Best wishes for your latest game and thank you again.

Alan Wahlers, Nottinghamshire, England.
CONGRATULATIONS!: The top three scorers will receive official Golden Logres T-shirts.
