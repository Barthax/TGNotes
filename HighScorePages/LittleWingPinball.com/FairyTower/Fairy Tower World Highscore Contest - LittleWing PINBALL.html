The Silver Prize: Akihiko Takahash!
Yaizu Shizuoka Japan
Score: 5,436,993,300

I'm very happy to become second in the world in LittleWing Fairy Tower highscore contest. I didn't expect to get the silver prize though temporarily I was in the first place during the contest period as I ended up as 16th in the Monster Fair contest after achieving the bronze prize in JINNI ZEALA contest.

I thought the slumping was caused by a decrease of my concentration power. I aged quite a bit since I started to play LittleWing pinball game with Tristan 20 years ago. I played all LittleWing pinball games. This time I spent days for one game taking breaks for every orb collecting. Sometimes I paused a game even in multiball play. Of course I was bracing myself for being overtaken in such a situation, it seems like a miracle to mark my best position .

Although the above might sound like an excuse, for sure my goal is the world champion whenever any new table is released. I'll pursue my dream for life no matter how old I get. Thank you.
